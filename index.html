Daniela Ferreiro, Ph.D. Student

About me

I am research student working under the supervison of Manuel Hermenegildo and José F. Morales at the IMDEA Software Institute. I am part of the development team of the Ciao programming language, focused on the abstract interpreter of its preprocessor, CiaoPP.
I hold a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics and Computer Science from the Technical University of Madrid and a Master's degree in Formal Methods from the {Complutense, Autonomous, Polytechnic} University of Madrid.

Research Interests
Abstract Interpretation; Static Analysis, Verification, and Testing of Software; (Constraint) Logic Programming; Programming Language Design and Implementation.
